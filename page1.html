What happened in 2012?
Let's explore what happened to the victims of 2012 who got killed in drunk driving based on the geo map of United States.
As a first step, lets get an verview of the chart that represents two kinds of causes for deaths.
1. Total Fatlities:119,100
Total fatalities is a chloropeth map with divering RED and BLUE colors that represent number of people killed in fatalities by State. The lowest represented by dark blue and the higest represented by RED. Legend shows the threshold of each color. Most populated states like Texas, California and Florida and most of eastern side of United States reported the highest number of fatalities.
2. Total Prevalence:1.9%
Prevalence is the number of people reoprted driving after too much drinking. This is %, rate per 100 population represented in yellow bubbles. The biggger the bubble, the prevalence is greater. Legend shows the threshold for each circle.

What are the user interactions available?
Tooltip can be used to know the fatalities and prevalence by each state. Can navigate to next screen using the buttons on the navigation bar.

What does the story tell ?
States that are red/orange reported most fatalities. States that are blue i.e. which did not show too many fatalities on the contrary reported highest prevalence.
That means, drunk drivers are everywhere costing the lives of many!!
